# Privacy Policy

**ASS - ASCII Smuggling Surfacer**

**Data Collection and Usage**
ASS - ASCII Smuggling Surfacer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, browsing history, or user data. 

**Local Processing**
All webpage analysis, detection of hidden characters, and decoding of steganographic payloads is performed entirely locally on the user's device. No data is ever sent to external servers or third parties.

**Permissions Used**
* **activeTab:** Used to access the text content of the currently active tab when the user explicitly interacts with the extension in order to scan for hidden characters.
* **storage:** Used exclusively to save the user's local preferences (like chosen visual themes and auto-calming thresholds) on the device.
* **scripting / sidePanel:** Used to execute the local detection logic to visually highlight hidden text directly within the webpage and show the analysis in the side panel.

**Contact**
For any issues or questions, please refer to the project's repository.
